Welcome to Brooklyn Bridge

Hey! This is more than just a bridge! This famous sight is the background for many movies, romantic engagement proposals, everyday bikers, and many tourist! Take a break from classes and take in the breeze of the East River with a stroll down the Brooklyn Bridge.

Welcome to New York Campus

Right smack in the middle of the city! NYU in New York’s campus is the center for diversity, creativity, and scholar. This urban setting transports you from the classroom to the bustling city within seconds. Find dining halls, restaurants, coffee shops, and various academic buildings spread throughout the campus.

Welcome to the Washington Square

“Everybody gather around” Sorry, that is just the sound of street performers gathering a crowd for their daily performace in Washington Square Park. This park doubles as NYU campus quad. However, not your typical quad. This park is filled with performers, rallies, food carts, a large water fountain you can splash around in and everyone’s favorite: the bird man!

Welcome to The Bund

Cautious! This is one of the most crowded place in Shanghai! But, it is not to miss! The views give you a picturesque view of the city and many nightlife options! The Bund is only 2 stops away from the NYU campus, offering a quick commute! The iconic Oriental Pearl Tower lights up in purple in celebration of NYU Shanghai graduating class each year!

Welcome to the Dorm

Located conveniently around many restaurants, shopping, and the Jinqiao train station a block away. A mix of old and new Shanghai allows you to witness the ever-changing aspects of this new city. The rooms are all brand new and provide access to cooking ware, oven, stove, fridge, and both a washer and dryer! Jinqiao Dorms is your one stop shop for the perfect living atmosphere!

Welcome to the Shanghai Campus

You’ve arrived to the fanciest part of Pudong! Located in the financial area of Shanghai you can take in views of the Oriental Pearl Tower, Shanghai Tower, and the Bottle Opener building. Although one building, the school is filled with an abundance of diversity, variety of majors, and an array of activities. This campus features the Interactive Media Arts major that is special to just this NYU campus.

Welcome to the Mosque

This famous mosque is a major attraction and iconic sight that identifies Abu Dhabi. Take a trip to the mosque to immerse yourself into the culture and religion of the city and take in the beauties of the pristine and clean architecture.

Welcome to the Dorm

Live luxurious! NYU Abu Dhabi provides you with free bedding, towels, and other necessities that you can take home with you after your stay on campus! Enjoy the clean, new, and comfortable amenities! Have a wonderful stay!

Welcome to AD's Campus

Why not study in the Middle East? You won’t feel like you are in the desert here in Abu Dhabi. Brand new buildings with state of the art facilities is calling your name! Did we mention it’s always warm! You will want to study outside on the gorgeous grounds of this campus!
